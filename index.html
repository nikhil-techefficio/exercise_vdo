India is striving to become the world's 3rd largest economy by 2027 and a developed nation by 2047, necessitating sustained economic growth of 6.5% annually until 2027 and 8-9% thereafter. Small businesses will be crucial in this effort. Currently, Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) contribute around 30% to India’s GDP, account for 43.6% of merchandise exports, and provide nearly 123 million jobs. The key is for Indian entrepreneurs, known for their innovation and problem-solving, to rapidly scale up their small businesses, driving grassroots growth.

In this evolving landscape, Forbes India introduces ‘The Small Business Summit,’ aimed at becoming the largest platform for small businesses in India. As a precursor to the Summit, Forbes India will host SMB Huddle events in Bangalore, Pune, Chennai, and Chandigarh, culminating in the main Summit in Delhi.
